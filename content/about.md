# About Me

Hello! I'm a blogger who believes in the power of simple, fast, and accessible web content.

## This Blog

This blog is built with:

- **[Hugo](https://gohugo.io/)**: A fast static site generator
- **[Bear Blog Theme](https://github.com/janraasch/hugo-bearblog)**: A minimal, lightweight theme inspired by [Bear Blog](https://bearblog.dev/)

## Philosophy

I believe the web should be:

- **Fast**: No one likes waiting for pages to load
- **Accessible**: Everyone should be able to access content regardless of their device or connection speed
- **Privacy-respecting**: No tracking, no ads, no unnecessary scripts
- **Content-focused**: Good writing should be the star, not flashy designs

Feel free to reach out if you have any questions or just want to connect!
